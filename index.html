Berlin Passages

Welcome to this autofictional interactive journey through Berlin's past and present!

To start, click on the specific chapter, and it will automatically open in a new tab.

Desktop version is recommended over mobile.

You do not need to install any software to start playing.

Chapters:

  1. Intro and Relocation
  2. Discovery
  3. Occupation
  4. Bonding
  5. Celebration
  6. Neglection
  7. Nostalgie
  8. Reconnection
  9. (extra) Club Navigator Mini Game

Every chapter takes around 15 to 20 minutes to read.

If you want to go through the story more quickly, you can read chapters 1, 2, 5, 7, and 8.

But, reading all the chapters will provide the complete experience.
